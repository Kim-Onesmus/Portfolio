Why need a website

-A website is a collection of webpages located under a single domain name.
-The webpages contain and display information about the business or organization and are usually made up of elements such as photos, videos and text.

Importants of a website
1. Without a website, people may question your legitimacy as a business.
2. Having a website is an opportunity to make a great first impression and give people comfort that you have a real business.
3. Showcasing your brand to your prospective customers via a website is one of the most important things that you can do.
4. Having a website makes you look professional and increases trust.
5. Most people don't trust a business without a good website.
6. A clean, modern page that is regularly updated and pops up in search engines is an instant credibility boost
